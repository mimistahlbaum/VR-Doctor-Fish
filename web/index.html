VR Doctor Fish

A relaxing virtual fish therapy session, right in your browser. Water flows around your legs, small fish nibble at your feet, a big fish takes a bite, jellyfish drift past, then everything calms down.

Headphones recommended. Best paired with a real bucket of water for your feet. On a Meta Quest browser you can enter VR after starting.
