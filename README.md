# VR Doctor Fish

**Experience a relaxing, virtual fish therapy from the comfort of your home.**

VR Doctor Fish recreates a doctor fish foot spa in virtual reality. The user wears a VR headset while coordinated visual, audio and haptic feedback simulates fish gently nibbling at their feet.

## How it works

- **VR display** – Meta Quest 3 presents the virtual foot spa environment
- **Haptics** – VibraForge actuators simulate the nibbling sensation. Strong and weak actuators are combined with contrasting texture patterns (low intensity / high frequency and high intensity / low frequency) to vary the feel
- **Audio** – background music and sound effects reinforce the visual and haptic experience
